**[0:15 - 0:40] The Solution & Landing Page (Screen Record: Scroll down Landing Page)**

As you can see by our enterprise-grade 3D landing page, we built this to be trusted by the biggest platforms."

Instantly, our proprietary spatial-temporal AI verifies the live GPS telemetry of active riders to suppress fraud... and boom. The engine executes a ₹400 multi-sig payout directly to the affected workers' UPI accounts in under 15 milliseconds."

"GigShield is intelligent, instant, and completely automated. This isn’t just an app; it’s financial infrastructure for the backbone of India. Thank you for reviewing our DEVTrails 2026 hackathon submission."
